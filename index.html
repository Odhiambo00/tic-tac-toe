Game Rules

Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game where the goal is to get three of your marks in a row.

Players take turns placing their "X" or "O" in an empty cell of the 3x3 grid.

The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
